## Usage

* Use when a user must choose one option from a predefined set.
* Ensure all options are clearly labeled and easy to understand.
* Use the customizable content to provide additional context when needed.
* Group related radio buttons together with a clear heading

> **Note:** For details on sizes, behaviors, and general guidelines applicable to all form components, refer to the [Form Field](https://zeroheight.com/98bb1df01/p/6854b6-form-field) documentation.

## Desktop usage and treatment

Radio groups on desktop should provide clear visual feedback for hover and focus states. The options should be well-spaced and easily clickable, with consistent alignment and clear visual hierarchy.

## Mobile usage and treatment

On mobile devices, radio groups should have adequate touch targets and clear visual feedback for selection states. Consider the reduced screen space when laying out options and ensure they remain easily accessible for touch interactions.
