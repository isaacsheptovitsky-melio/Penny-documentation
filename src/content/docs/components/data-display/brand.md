## Use cases

* **Product pages**: Display partner or payment method logos for credibility
* **Checkout flow**: Show payment method logos for clear options
* **Partner listings**: Highlight partner logos to emphasize collaborations

## Accessibility

* Ensure color contrast
* Provide alt text when logos are not decorative
* Do not rely solely on logos to convey information
