## Accessibility

- Ensure sufficient **color contrast** between the badge and its background
- Avoid relying on **color alone** to convey meaning—use icons or text when possible

## Desktop usage and treatment

Use badges to provide quick visual indicators for status, verification, or other important metadata. Position badges consistently to maintain visual hierarchy and ensure they don't interfere with the primary content.

## Mobile usage and treatment

On mobile devices, ensure badges remain legible and appropriately sized for touch interactions. Consider the reduced screen space and prioritize the most critical badge information for display.
