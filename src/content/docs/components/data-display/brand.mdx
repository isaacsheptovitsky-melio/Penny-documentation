## Usage

Use the Brand component to show partner, product, or payment logos

<Aside type="tip">
  Use the **Neutral** variant when you want to de-emphasize the logo without losing its brand recognition, such as in non-prominent UI elements.
</Aside>

## Use cases

- **Product pages**: Display partner or payment method logos for credibility
- **Checkout flow**: Show payment method logos for clear options
- **Partner listings**: Highlight partner logos to emphasize collaborations

## Accessibility

- Ensure color contrast
- Provide alt text when logos are not decorative
- Do not rely solely on logos to convey information

## Desktop usage and treatment

Brand components should be prominently displayed where brand recognition and trust are important. Maintain appropriate spacing and sizing to ensure logos are clearly visible and recognizable.

## Mobile usage and treatment

On mobile devices, consider the reduced screen space and prioritize the most important brand elements. Ensure logos remain legible at smaller sizes while maintaining their brand integrity.
