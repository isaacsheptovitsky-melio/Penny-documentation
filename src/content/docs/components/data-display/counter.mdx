## Usage

- Show how many items belong to a category (e.g. "Invoices (4)")
- Indicate filtered or selected item counts
- Display totals in tabs, pills, or section headers

<Aside type="tip">
  Pair counters with clear labels to avoid confusion, context is key
</Aside>

## Desktop usage and treatment

Counters work effectively in navigation tabs, section headers, and category labels where users need quick numerical context. Position them consistently near their associated labels for clear association.

## Mobile usage and treatment

On mobile devices, ensure counters remain visible and readable. Consider their placement in condensed layouts and maintain adequate touch targets when counters are interactive.
